Mickey Mouse goes to school to become a plane pilot. In this read along story book for children I read aloud. Minnie Mouse, Donald Duck, Goofy, Daisy and Pluto all miss him as he spends more and more time at the airport learning to fly. After spending lots of time flying planes Mickey goes on his first solo flight and ends up discovering his friends having a picnic. He lands the plane and enjoys the picnic with them then takes them each on their first plane ride!
